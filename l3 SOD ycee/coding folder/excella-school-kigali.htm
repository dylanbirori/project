Excella School

Excella School is located in Kimironko. The school has a nursery, Primary and Secondary Levels available. And for all levels the main teaching language is English.

The Nursery and Primary Sections exclusively follow the Cambridge Curriculum. But The Secondary Level has both Rwanda National Curriculum. Up to very recently, the school was an accredited Cambridge school, but it seems like the school has now introduced American Montessori as a new curriculum. Since September 2020, Excella Is no longer a Cambridge School.
